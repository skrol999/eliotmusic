About

Nine Live Music is an artist management and music strategy consultancy founded by Eliot Schul. We work with artists, labels, publishers, and management companies that need both creative vision and operational rigour.

With over 6 years spanning PR, tour management, publishing, label operations, marketing, and A&R, Eliot brings a genuinely complete perspective to every project. That cross-disciplinary background means strategy that's grounded in how the industry actually works — at every level, across every department. From the creative room to the boardroom.

Whether managing artists through their development or advising labels on release campaigns and creative camps, the goal is always the same: build something that lasts.
